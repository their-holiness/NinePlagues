NinePlagues


What is NinePlagues?

NinePlagues is a quiz, running on a modded base of AltValues (originally SexValues), that attempts to diagnose your spiritual illnesses and suggest practices to solve them see the ninefold vaccine for more info. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will becompared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the values?

There are nine plagues. They are:


Alienation
Disconnection from other people. The worst plague because it keeps you from getting help from others.

Angst
Disconnection from yourself, inner conflict

Absurdity
Disconnection from the world, feeling like it is meaningless

Forgetting
Forgetting wonder and trying to fulfill your spiritual needs with physical things

Spiralling
Focusing too much on something to the detriment of other things

Drowning
Inability to think through your actions, overfocusing on things to the point of paralysis or not focusing enough

Foreign entrapment
Applying ideas that worked at one point to situations they don't work in. For example attachment styles.

Ego entrapment
Being afraid to change because it would challenge your self image

Empathic entrapment
Being unable or unwilling to explore new perspectives

What is "Diagnosis" in the results?

This test tries to analyze your plague profile and suggest a treatment for you, it is not perfect and will need adjustment in the future and possibly a complete overhaul of the 8values inherited system.

Privacy

This site uses Google Analytics. As well at the end of the test, you will be given the option to fill out general demographic information about yourself. There will be 3 options. The first will take you to a small survey and will record your answers to that along with the results. The second will just record your results. The third will not record anything. All information recorded is totally anonymous.
